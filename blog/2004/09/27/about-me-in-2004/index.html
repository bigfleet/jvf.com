About Me in 2004

by Jim Van Fleet - Sep 27th, 2004 -

What time do you get up? Depends on my schedule for the day, never later than 8.
If you could eat lunch with one person who would it be? Mark Cuban, who made a billion on the Internet, got out while the getting was good, then bought a sports franchise. I’d also probably hit him up for some money.
Gold or Silver? Gold
What was the last film you saw at the cinema? Napoleon Dynamite. Get down!
What is/are your favorite TV shows? Six Feet Under.
What did you have for breakfast? Special K and cranberry juice.
Who would you hate to be stuck in a room with? Somebody dead? Stinky!
What is your middle name? Alanson
Beach, City, Country or Mountains? Beach.
Favorite Ice Cream? Friendly’s Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup concoction.
Butter, Plain or salted popcorn? Small soda.
Favorite color? Something like this.
 
What kind of car do you drive? Silver 2000 Honda CRV
Favorite Sandwich? Oh, man… Meatbull sub from Zero’s, Spicy Monterrey Club from Quizno’s, or Prime Rib sandwich from Enzo’s.
What characteristic do you despise? Self-righteousness.
Favorite flower? Tulip.
If you could go anywhere in the world on vacation, where would you go? Australia.
What color is your bathroom? Walls are all white, I’ve added some blues and wicker baskets.
Favorite Brand of clothing? Polo and Tommy Hilfiger. It’s because they fit, dammit!
Where would you retire? Wilmington, NC
Favorite Day of the week? Saturday, Sunday, then pay day.
What did you do for your last Birthday? I can’t remember.
Where were you born? Knoxville, TN
Favorite sport to watch on TV? Football
Who do you least expect to send this back to you? I don’t really think anyone will…
Person you expect to send back first? … except maybe Elizabeth.
What fabric detergent do you use? Gain.
Coke or Pepsi? Pepsi.
Are you a morning person or night owl? I hit my peak around 2PM.
What is your shoe size? 13
Do you have any pets? Yes, three cats. Whisps, Riley, and Sophia.
Who sent this to you? Allie Farnum
