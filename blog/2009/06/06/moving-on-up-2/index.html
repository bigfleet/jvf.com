Moving on Up

by Jim Van Fleet - Jun 6th, 2009 -

What do these things have in common?
  • Shopping for a house
  • Making an offer on a house
  • Having the offer accepted
  • Getting financed
  • Closing on the house
  • Moving in
  • Unpacking
Well, they have multiple things in common, but the one I’m thinking of is that I apparently do not consider them blogworthy. I will now shamelessly steal from my wife’s blog to show off.
