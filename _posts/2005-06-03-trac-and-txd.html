I have recently fallen in love with Trac, as it combines a Wiki, an excellent bug tracker, some rudimentary project controls, and a source code browser into one package.

I already fell in love with TextDrive where my VCII status gives me shared hosting for life.

Now with Running Trac on TxD, I can combine the two. You should check them both out!
