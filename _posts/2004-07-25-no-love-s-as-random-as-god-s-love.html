I can’t stand that the entire process by which a candidate becomes president in this country can be something that’s analyzed to the depth that you and I can read articles about it on the Internet.

What’s left? A lot of highly specific, specialized strategy to “win”, to get your candidate into the White House.

When did this stop being about the right thing? When did this stop being about America?
