In a piece he calls The End of the Knowledge Worker?, Dave Thomas talks about about what it’s like for a programmer to progress through their professional life, and what the greater meaning in that process for the industry.

I think it’s quite insightful, and I also think it’s clear that it means the “unskilled” programmers will be the first to be outsourced. Isn’t this what we want? Does the world need another poorly written app?
